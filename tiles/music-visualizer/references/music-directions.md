Choose one listening job and one visual family before writing the music prompt.
Keep the first sample 45–90 seconds; scale to 10 minutes only after the sample
passes a calm-motion review.

## Prompt rules

- Describe musical properties, not living artists, songs, or copyrighted lyrics.
- State the use case: deep work, reading, coffee shop, evening reflection, or
  sleep preparation.
- Specify instrumentation, energy range, section shape, and exclusions.
- For 10-minute pieces, ask for a cohesive structure with arrival, settling,
  variation, opening, return, and release; avoid obvious looping.
- Use light percussion only when the visual has enough negative space to absorb
  it. Never let a beat force mechanical geometry.
- For hand-painted pastoral or animated-film atmosphere, describe the palette,
  materials, orchestration, pacing, and emotional temperature. Never name a
  living director, composer, studio, song, or franchise as a style target.

## Archived visual failures

`Clay Atlas`, `Pigment Tide`, and `Paper Atlas` are archived experiments, not
selectable directions. They failed the visual bar because they reduced a scene
to a few generic shapes or texture fields and did not deliver a world, a clear
subject, or organic visual life. Do not revive them without Aaron explicitly
asking to revisit one.

## Recommended sample prompts

### Midnight Lo-Fi Wave

```text
Instrumental only. Warm lo-fi wave for deep work and coding: mellow Rhodes
electric piano, soft sub bass, dusty tape texture, restrained brushed kick and
snare, a quiet analog pulse, and spacious chord changes. Calm, hypnotic,
low-to-medium energy, no vocals, no bright lead, no abrupt drop, no harsh highs,
no busy hi-hat pattern. Structure: a short arrival, a settled groove, one small
harmonic opening, a restrained return, and a soft unresolved release.
```

### Coffee Room

```text
Instrumental only. Warm coffee-room background music for reading: intimate
upright piano, brushed percussion, muted jazz chords, soft bass, subtle room
ambience, and a gently repeating motif. Unhurried, human, quietly satisfying,
low dynamic range. No vocals, no dramatic solo, no abrupt climax, no bright
arpeggios, no trailer impacts.
```

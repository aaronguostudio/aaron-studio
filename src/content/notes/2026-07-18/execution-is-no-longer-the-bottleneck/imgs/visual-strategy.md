# Visual Strategy: Execution Is No Longer the Bottleneck

## Article character

Compact reflective argument about the shift from costly execution to an idea-experiment-system loop.

## Reader emotion

Clarity, possibility, momentum, and calm agency.

## Visual risk

The topic easily collapses into a generic AI workflow diagram, glowing network, or dashboard. Keep the system metaphor tactile and human-scale.

## Concept routes

1. **Editorial workbench** — a rough paper idea becomes several small material prototypes, then settles into one coherent modular structure across a sunlit table. Predicate: an idea becomes experiments, and experiments become a repeatable system.
2. **Branch and trellis** — one paper seed produces several translucent shoots that converge into a stable wooden trellis. Predicate: exploration becomes supporting structure.
3. **Workshop jig** — a loose handmade object passes through several small test fixtures and emerges as a reusable tool. Predicate: repeated trials create repeatable capability.

## Selection

`Agent-selected`: Route 1, Editorial workbench. It is the clearest expression of the Note, continues the warm desk world of the first Builder Note, and avoids literal AI imagery.

## Final predicate

A rough idea becomes several small experiments, and the learning from those experiments becomes one coherent system.

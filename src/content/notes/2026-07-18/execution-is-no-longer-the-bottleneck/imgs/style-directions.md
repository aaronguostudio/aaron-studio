# Style Directions

## Menu

1. **Editorial Workbench + Soft Glass Narrative** — premium sunlit still life, tactile paper and wood, restrained cyan system thread. Best continuity with Builder Notes. Risk: becoming generic desk photography.
2. **Field Signal Editorial** — flat paper-cut composition with graphite structure and authored pencil marks. More explicitly editorial. Risk: feeling like a diagram.
3. **Abstract Mechanism** — minimal geometry showing divergence and convergence. Strong at thumbnail size. Risk: generic abstraction.

## Decision

`Agent-selected`: Unified Editorial Workbench with restrained Soft Glass Narrative accents. The concept should read as a crafted editorial still life, not a workflow slide.

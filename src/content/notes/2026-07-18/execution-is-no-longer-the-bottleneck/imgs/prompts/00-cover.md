# Prompt: 00-cover

## Role

Builder Note clean cover and social master.

## Visual Mode

Editorial Minimal.

## Visual Weight

Medium.

## Style Family

Editorial Workbench with restrained Soft Glass Narrative accents.

## Positive Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept. Asset type: clean 16:9 editorial blog cover. Image 1 is a style and mood reference only; do not copy its composition or objects. Create a premium sunlit editorial still life on a calm natural-wood workbench. Show one rough folded paper or sketch-like blank card at the beginning, three distinct small tactile prototypes in the middle, and one elegant coherent modular structure at the end. A single thin soft-cyan thread quietly travels through the stages, suggesting delegated AI execution and accumulated learning. Warm white plaster background, pale wood, graphite and stone objects, restrained translucent acrylic, one tiny amber accent, natural morning light, generous negative space, subtle material texture. The image should communicate: an idea becomes experiments, then experiments become a reusable system.

## Negative Prompt

No readable text, letters, numbers, arrows, labels, screens, charts, robots, brains, clouds, logos, watermark, people, hands, neon, dark blue AI palette, sci-fi HUD, dashboard, isometric SaaS scene, excessive glow, excessive glass, or clutter.

## Mode Constraints

Exactly one left-to-right transformation and no more than three visual stage groups. The cyan thread is secondary.

## Density Budget

Narrative metaphor, minimal density.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Do not reproduce the empty desk/rest composition of the reference image. This cover needs quiet transformation, not inactivity.

# Audio Material Studies v1

Two 45-second, 1080p / 30fps audio-reactive prototypes. Both use the 15–60s
window of the original `Prism Breaks Long Night` master used in the published
Obsidian Relay study. The window was re-analysed before rendering: 1,351
precomputed audio frames drive the visuals deterministically.

| Study | Audio mapping | Verdict |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ferrofield | Low band → viscosity and field depth; highs → rim light; transient → one internal aperture bloom. | A useful material direction, but needs a second visual pass before it becomes a lead format. |
| Glass Orbit | Low band → spatial depth and floor light; highs → glass-edge light; transient → one aperture bloom. | Stronger first prototype; its audio response is legible without destabilising the space. |

Each folder contains the source-audio window, analysis, render props, manifest,
contact sheet, and a final MP4 with the original audio muxed into it. No image
assets, generative image model, wall-clock animation, beat-driven translation,
or scale bounce is used.

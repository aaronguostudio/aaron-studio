# Verdant Cloud Study v1 — visual QA

Status: approved as a **visual prototype**, pending Aaron's creative review.

- Camera: distant, slightly elevated three-quarter bird's-eye view; no close-up or camera travel.
- Figure: complete head, torso, arms, and seated pose remain in frame across the sampled sequence.
- Staging: laptop is placed in front of and facing the seated figure; the viewer sees its back/side rather than an implausibly viewer-facing work screen.
- Environment: a self-contained green island and wide cloud ocean carry the composition; no bare canyon, cropped foreground desk, buildings, or poles.
- Motion: cloud, grass, and figure micro-motion remain restrained in the sampled frames.
- Seam: strict same-image first/last anchoring was used; sampled first and final frames retain the same framing and spatial relationships.
- Frame check: sampled first/final-frame PSNR is 41.74 dB, a strong match for a generated loop candidate.

This is intentionally silent for review. Do not extend or pair with the ten-minute soundtrack until the visual direction is selected.

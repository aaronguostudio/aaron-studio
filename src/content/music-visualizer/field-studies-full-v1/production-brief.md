# Field Studies — Rain Harbor

**Listening job:** rain-darkened deep work; observant rather than dramatic.

**Visual arc:**

1. Rain Harbor — wet concrete, distant amber light, black water.
2. Tidal Channel — a quieter, closer waterline with a low breakwater and mist.
3. Estuary Night — an open stone embankment and sparse remote lights after rain.

Each chapter is 202 seconds. The two chapter handoffs are three-second dissolves;
the camera is locked in every loop. There is no pan, no scale pulse, no vehicle,
and no foreground action.

**Music arc:** 4 independent Lyria Pro sections in D minor, 72 BPM, restrained
electric piano, low synth and brushed texture. The emotional change is only a
small opening of harmonic colour in the middle, then a return to near silence.

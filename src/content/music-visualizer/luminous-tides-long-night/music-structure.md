# Luminous Tides — long-form music structure

| Time | Musical role | Production treatment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0:00–1:30 | Arrival | Sparse Rhodes, distant tape air, no urgency |
| 1:30–4:00 | Settle | Stable focus groove and soft analog bass pulse |
| 4:00–5:24 | Opening | A small harmonic color and restrained secondary texture |
| 5:24–5:30 | Handoff | Six-second equal-power crossfade between original movements |
| 5:30–7:00 | Second movement | Darker continuation, intentionally no fresh intro |
| 7:00–8:30 | Gentle variation | Quiet shimmer and bass variation |
| 8:30–10:00 | Release | Texture thins into a warm unresolved close |

The final 10-minute master combines two separately generated original
instrumentals because the music generator returned a ~5:30 cap for a single
600-second request. It uses a six-second audio crossfade rather than a loop.

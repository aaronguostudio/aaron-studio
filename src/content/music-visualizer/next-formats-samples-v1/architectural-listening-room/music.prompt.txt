Instrumental only. A quiet architectural listening room for concentrated work:
low-register felt piano, warm wood resonance, soft room tone, a restrained
ambient pad, and distant rain texture. Around 72 BPM without a pronounced beat.
Measured, spacious, contemporary, and deeply calm. No vocals, no melodic hook,
no dramatic crescendo, no trailer impact, no busy rhythm. Let one subtle warm
chord opening pass through the middle before returning to cool stillness.

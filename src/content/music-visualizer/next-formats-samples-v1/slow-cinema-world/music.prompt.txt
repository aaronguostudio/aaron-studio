Instrumental only. Quiet pastoral ambient for reading: intimate felt piano with
a sparse unhurried motif, a distant breathy woodwind color, very soft sustained
strings, and natural room air. About 68 BPM with no explicit drum groove. Warm,
open, contemplative, and gently hopeful. No vocals, no heroic swell, no trailer
impact, no virtuoso solo, no abrupt chord change. A subtle harmonic lift near the
middle returns to stillness at the end.

Instrumental only. A quiet blue-hour harbor field study for deep work: muted
electric piano fragments, soft warm analog bass, distant rain-like texture, a
very restrained brushed pulse, and large open space. 78 BPM, nocturnal,
observant, low-to-medium energy. No vocals, no bright lead, no drop, no snare
fill, no busy hi-hats, no dramatic climax. Let a small amber harmonic reflection
arrive halfway through and dissolve gently.

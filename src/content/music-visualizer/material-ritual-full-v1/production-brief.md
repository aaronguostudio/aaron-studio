# Material Ritual — Glass and Water

**Listening job:** precise, soft focus for close work.

**Visual arc:**

1. Amber Lens — a clear sphere over dark water and one warm internal droplet.
2. Smoke Basin — a thin smoked-glass disc above a shallow slate basin.
3. Porcelain Tide — pale porcelain, blue-black water and one restrained copper
   reflection.

Every chapter is a physical tabletop study, not an abstract graphic. The object
does not pulse, breathe, rotate or change shape; a moving light trace and a
slowly returning water ripple make the loop alive. Chapters are 202 seconds
with two three-second dissolves.

**Music arc:** 4 independent Lyria Pro sections in A minor, 78 BPM, glassy
mallets, dry piano, soft sub warmth and almost inaudible brushed texture. No
bright arpeggio, beat drop, voice, sparkle effect or aggressive transient.

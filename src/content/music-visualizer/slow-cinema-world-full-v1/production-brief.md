# Slow Cinema World — Cloud Observatory

**Listening job:** spacious reading and unhurried reflection.

**Visual arc:**

1. Cloud Observatory — the original grass island, shallow pool and weather dome.
2. Weather Shelf — a low cedar shelter above a rolling pale cloud shelf.
3. Evening Beacon — a smaller island observatory at blue dusk, still held inside
   a wide cloud ocean.

The world remains uninhabited. Chapters are 202 seconds, joined by two quiet
three-second dissolves. Clouds, grass, water and a slow warmth in the light are
the only moving elements.

**Music arc:** 4 independent Lyria Pro sections in G major / E minor, 64 BPM,
felt piano, an airy woodwind-like synth and quiet string pads. No melody-led
climax, vocals, percussion breaks or cinematic swell.

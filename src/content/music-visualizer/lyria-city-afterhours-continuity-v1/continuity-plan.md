# Lyria continuity experiment

Three independently generated Lyria Pro sections share a fixed key, tempo, and
instrumentation. The access-check generation acts as the opening; the second
and third prompts preserve the shared sonic palette while changing the internal
movement. They are joined with two six-second equal-power crossfades, then
normalized once for listening.

This is a continuity experiment, not a claim that Lyria natively extends audio.

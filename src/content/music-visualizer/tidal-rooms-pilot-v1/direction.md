# Tidal Rooms

Listening job: quiet reading or late-afternoon deep work.

Visual spine: five coastal thresholds, moving from misty rock and tide-pool detail to a high, architectural coast and a low, quiet pier. The edit uses static images and slow dissolves; the final frame alone eases outward by one percent.

Sound: original Google Lyria instrumental in D minor at 84 BPM — felt piano, muted synth bass, glassy tones, and a restrained pulse.

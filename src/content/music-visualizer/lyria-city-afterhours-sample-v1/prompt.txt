Instrumental only, no vocals. A poised 30-second electronic night piece for focused work in a quiet modern city after rain: 100 BPM, a restrained analog bass pulse, soft clipped percussion, warm muted chord stabs, a sparse glassy arpeggio, and wide low-lit space. Begin with the bass and soft atmosphere, add a subtle rhythmic lift in the middle, then finish with a calm resolved final bar. Elegant, tactile, nocturnal, structured, and gently cinematic. Avoid lo-fi hip-hop, EDM drops, four-on-the-floor dance beats, bright supersaw leads, aggressive kick drums, overly busy fills, and sudden endings.

Instrumental only. A warm, spacious desert-focus piece for reading and unhurried work. 92 BPM in D minor. Nylon-string guitar plays sparse, original motifs over soft hand percussion, a rounded acoustic bass, airy sustained synth, and a very small amount of distant wind-like texture. The color is sun-warmed ochre, chalk, and deep blue shadow. Low-to-medium energy, patient phrasing, long rests, no vocals, no recognizable melody, no dramatic build, no trailer drums, no harsh cymbals, no busy arpeggios. Shape: arrive in open air, settle into a quiet pulse, make one gentle harmonic widening, then return to stillness.

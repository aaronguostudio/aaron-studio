# Architectural Listening Room — Rain Garden

**Listening job:** calm architectural ambience for writing and late reading.

**Visual arc:**

1. Rain Garden — warm timber, concrete, one chair and a square garden opening.
2. Fog Court — an empty reading alcove with a broad opening onto a wet courtyard.
3. Pool Pavilion — a circular concrete shelter looking onto a quiet foggy pool.

The built space stays still. Only rain, distant foliage, fog and soft reflected
light change. No inhabitants, screens, decorative text, camera motion, or
furniture movement. Each chapter is 202 seconds with two three-second dissolves.

**Music arc:** 4 independent Lyria Pro sections in C major / A minor, 66 BPM,
low piano, warm timber resonance and faint room tone. No vocal, beat drop,
dramatic swell or treble-heavy rain imitation.

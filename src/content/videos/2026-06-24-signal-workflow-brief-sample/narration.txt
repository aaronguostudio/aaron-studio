Signal flagged a Perplexity update this week. The headline is Deep Research moving deeper into Computer.

That sounds like a product announcement. But for operators, the question is simpler: does this remove a step from a real workflow?

Take a market brief. The old flow is search, save sources, summarize, compare, write, then review.

The new promise is closer to: run the research, branch the useful thread, take inline actions, and keep the source trail attached.

My verdict: use it for first-pass research briefs. Do not use it as the final answer machine.

The value is speed to structured material, not outsourcing judgment.

Next, I would test it against Perplexity search plus a normal document workflow. Same brief, same sources, stopwatch on.

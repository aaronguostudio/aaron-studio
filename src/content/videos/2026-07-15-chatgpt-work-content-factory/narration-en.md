# A Content Factory That Keeps You in Control

## [HOOK]

Every morning, the same work starts again.

## [SLIDE: Keep the decisions — narration]

Scan the news, compare product updates, and decide what deserves a video. A content factory should not remove your judgment. It should remove the repetition. Schedule one task to collect signals and rank five ideas. Then stop for approval. Once you choose the topic, the system builds a fact pack, writes the script, plans every asset, and renders a draft. Then it stops again for QA. The machine keeps moving. You keep the decisions that matter.

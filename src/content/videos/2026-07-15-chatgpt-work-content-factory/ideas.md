## Selected MVP

**ChatGPT Work：每天自动找选题，等你批准后再生产**

This is the strongest first test because the product capability and Aaron's
content-factory idea are the same story. The Short can demonstrate a bounded
workflow instead of promising fully autonomous publishing:

```text
scheduled discovery -> ranked ideas -> human approval -> fact pack -> script
-> asset plan -> render -> QA -> human approval
```

The angle is not “ChatGPT has a new feature.” The angle is “use the feature to
move repetitive execution around two explicit human judgment points.”

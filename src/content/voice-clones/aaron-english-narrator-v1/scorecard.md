## Rubric

- Identity (5): Sounds recognizably like Aaron without preserving uncomfortable pronunciation artifacts.
- Naturalness (5): Feels like a person explaining an idea, not a synthetic read-through.
- Clarity (5): Technical terms are easy to understand on laptop and phone speakers.
- Pacing (5): Has useful pauses and does not rush dense passages.
- Listener fatigue (5): Can be heard for five minutes without strain, harshness, or repetitive cadence.

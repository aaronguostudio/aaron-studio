## Continuity thesis

The three earlier posts describe a progression at the individual level: AI as amplifier → persistent partner → personal operating system. This essay moves one level up: when do those personal changes become organizational and social change? It should preserve Aaron's builder credibility while adding historical discipline, counterarguments, and a public-interest lens.

## What not to repeat

- Do not re-explain basic prompting or list tools.
- Do not claim that “taste” alone is a universal AI moat.
- Do not turn the piece into a celebration of personal productivity; the point is how value, work, power, and opportunity move through a system.

# Idea: Curiosity Notes - The Farthest Humans Ever Went Started as a Failure

Series: 好奇心笔记 / Curiosity Notes

Seed:

Artemis II broke the human spaceflight distance record in April 2026, surpassing the mark set by Apollo 13. The surprising part is not only the new number. The old record came from a mission that failed to land on the Moon and became an emergency return.

Working angle:

The farthest point a system reaches is not always created by ambition. Sometimes it is created by failure, contingency, and the ability to improvise without losing the mission.

Column intent:

This should read as a knowledge essay, not an AI essay. The topic may be discovered through internet curiosity, but the article should stand on its own: fact, history, mechanism, and reflection. AI should not be the protagonist.

Draft title:

The Farthest Humans Ever Went Started as a Failure

Chinese title:

人类最远的距离，曾经来自一次失败

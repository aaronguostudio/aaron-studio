The Firm of One - Single Advisor Amplified by AI Agents

Minimalist hand-drawn line art in Notion style. Clean white background (#FFFFFF).

Layout: Hub-and-spoke diagram. One central human figure at the center, surrounded by AI agent cards radiating outward.

CENTER — The Human:
A simple stylized human silhouette seated at a small desk with a laptop. Not photorealistic — clean doodle outline. The person represents a single financial advisor. A subtle glow or circle radiates from the figure to connect to the spokes.

Human figures: simplified stylized silhouettes or symbolic representations, not photorealistic.

SPOKES (6–8 agent cards radiating outward from the central figure via dotted lines):
Each card is a small rounded rectangle with a tiny robot/agent icon and a short label:

1. Research Agent — small magnifying glass icon
2. Portfolio Review — small bar chart icon
3. Client Comms — small speech bubble icon
4. Reporting — small document icon
5. Compliance Check — small checkmark shield icon
6. Market Analysis — small trend line icon
7. Meeting Prep — small calendar icon
8. Risk Monitor — small gauge icon

Dotted lines connect the central figure to each agent card, creating a sun/spoke visual.

Below the entire diagram: small label text "One advisor. Many agents." in hand-drawn lettering.

COLORS: Black outlines (#1A1A1A) on white (#FFFFFF). Soft Blue (#A8D4F0) fills on agent cards. Pastel Yellow (#F9E79F) subtle glow ring around the central figure. Dotted lines in Slate Gray (#4A4A4A).

STYLE: Hand-drawn wobble effect. Simple doodle icons. Single-weight lines. Maximum whitespace. Text should be large and prominent with handwritten-style fonts. Keep minimal.

# Video Treatment V2: FDE Field Signal

## Decision

Keep the score, the sound-led opening, the dimensional model reveal, and the
progressive ACTOR build. Rebuild the visual system and high-risk layouts around
registered geometry rather than adding more effects.

## Visual Baseline

- Candidate system: `signal-editorial-v0.1`.
- Canvas: near-black neutral field.
- Primary accent: Signal jade for learning, progress, and retained capability.
- Secondary accent: coral only for handoff, gap, or unresolved tension.
- Paper is reserved for a model surface or recommendation artifact.
- Companies, system layers, and ACTOR cards no longer receive arbitrary colors.
- Grid: 112px horizontal safe area, consistent headers, equal peer dimensions,
  and a protected caption zone.

This palette is a versioned baseline to test across several videos, not a final
brand declaration.

## Layout Changes

### Opening

The first frame contains both the FDE title and a visible Model -> Field ->
Product scaffold. The diagram has a semantic job and replaces decorative route
lines.

### Four Companies

Four muted card positions are visible at scene entry and activate with the
narration. Before the central question appears, the cards leave and resolve
into a compact evidence rail. The question owns a separate stage.

### Deployment Gap

Keep the 3D surface turn. Replace multicolor operating cards with an unframed,
single-accent operating list. Coral appears only at the deployment gap.

### Consulting And FDE

Use equal columns, identical panel typography, shared node geometry, and one
full-width result band. Remove the floating `WHAT COMPOUNDS?` card.

### ACTOR

Use one framework-card component for all five letters. The recursive arrowhead
waits until the return path is effectively complete. The payoff enters in a
separate lower stage.

## Audio

- Preserve the approved Aaron PVC performance.
- Apply a transparent `1.04x` tempo pass to the narration.
- Retime word captions with the same ratio.
- Retime the approved music independently to the new 80.90-second master so the
  full musical arc remains intact.

## Acceptance Gate

- No title-only or empty structured scene entry.
- No dense evidence layout behind a large statement.
- Equal comparison lanes share dimensions, padding, and type.
- No arrowhead or destination precedes its parent path.
- Source and encoded-master contact sheets agree.
- Music remains the approved emotional spine and narration remains dominant.

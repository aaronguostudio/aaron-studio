# YouTube Script: If Models Are the Product, Why Spend Billions on People?

## [HOOK]

In fifty-nine days, Anthropic, OpenAI, A W S, and Microsoft made the same move:
they put engineers closer to customers. If models were the whole product, why
spend billions on people?

## [SLIDE: The Deployment Gap - deployment-gap.png]

Because a demo is not a working system. Models do not organize scattered data,
permissions, approvals, exceptions, and recovery. Closing that gap is where
forward deployed engineering begins.

## [SLIDE: Not Consulting With a New Title - consulting-fde.png]

F D E is not consulting with a new title. Consulting often ends with a
recommendation or handoff. A strong F D E team stays through production, fixes
failures beside operators, then returns reusable lessons to the product. What
matters is what compounds.

## [SLIDE: ACTOR Is My Deployment Framework - actor-framework.png]

ACTOR is part of my deployment framework. Action: what work changes? Context: what may
the system know? Trust: when may it recommend, decide, or act? Outcome: what
proves value? Recursive: how does this run improve the next one? Tokens are
inputs. Value appears when the workflow changes and the customer keeps the
capability.

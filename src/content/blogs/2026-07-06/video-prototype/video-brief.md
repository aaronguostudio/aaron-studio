# Video Brief: If Models Are the Product, Why Spend Billions on People?

This 60-90 second prototype tests whether the FDE article can feel like a
directed editorial film instead of an illustrated podcast. It compresses the
argument into one mystery, one distinction, and one usable framework.

## Target Audience

Technical builders, product leaders, and enterprise operators who believe AI
models are powerful but have seen good demonstrations fail to change real work.

## Desired Emotion

Curiosity in the opening, recognition in the comparison, and practical agency
when ACTOR appears.

## Core Promise

In roughly 75 seconds, explain why major AI companies are moving engineers
closer to customers, why FDE is not simply consulting with a new title, and how
ACTOR turns that deployment work into a practical framework for building operating capability.

## Title/Thumbnail Expectation

- Title: `If Models Are the Product, Why Spend Billions on People?`
- Subtitle: `The FDE deployment gap`
- Opening obligation: show the four-company convergence before the narration
  reaches its second sentence.

## High-Shock Facts

- 95: Anthropic, OpenAI, AWS, and Microsoft announced closely related
  deployment moves within 59 days.
- 91: The strategic response to enterprise AI friction includes putting more
  engineers near customer operations, not only releasing stronger models.
- 88: A strong FDE loop returns field failures to reusable product capability.

## Hook Type

Business mystery: if the model is the whole product, why are model companies
investing so heavily in people who work beside customers?

## Story Structure

Mystery -> deployment gap -> consulting objection -> ACTOR framework -> ownership
payoff.

## Retention Beat Map

- 0:00 - Four company names converge into one move.
- 0:14 - The polished model card opens to reveal the operational system behind it.
- 0:31 - Consulting hands off; FDE closes a field-to-product loop.
- 0:50 - ACTOR builds one element at a time.
- 1:12 - Tokens collapse into the customer-owned capability statement.

## What The Video Adds

- A sound-led opening rather than a thumbnail hold.
- A spatial reveal that turns a model product into a deployment system.
- A dynamic consulting/FDE comparison instead of a static table.
- A progressive ACTOR build synchronized to the narration.

## Banned Phrases

- let's dive in
- in today's video
- game changer
- here's the kicker
- this changes everything

## Ending

Land on the operating rule: Tokens are inputs; value appears when the workflow
changes and the customer keeps the capability.

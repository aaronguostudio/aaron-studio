# FDE Guizang-Hybrid Full Film: Director Memo

## Purpose

Create a private, full-length alternative to the published FDE film. The experiment keeps the approved V5 narration and music. It changes the visual grammar: a small editorial layout system, borrowed from the constraints of a strong presentation, is used to direct the film inside Remotion.

## What Changes

- Light paper, ink, cyan, coral, amber, and olive replace the persistent black-and-green application chrome.
- The film uses nine registered layout families rather than one-off compositions.
- Each chapter has an immediate, readable first frame. Dense layouts do not crossfade into each other.
- Cards establish before a connector can draw. A connector is never allowed to precede its source and target.
- Existing generated images appear only as editorial evidence or a quiet conceptual break. They do not replace authored explanations.

## Layout Families

1. Cover editorial
2. Announcement ledger
3. Scale signal and operating layer
4. Factory decision path
5. Field-learning loop
6. Workflow gates
7. Consulting versus FDE comparison
8. Customer-owned asset library
9. ACTOR framework and outcome statement

## Sound

Use the approved V5 narration (`R2DWp7zZuWmGxk3r8GIA`) and the approved cello-led score unchanged. The score opens above the narration, ducks when speech begins, and lifts only around ACTOR and the final operating rule.

## Private-Review Scope

This is not a publish candidate by default. It is a full visual proof: video, narration, captions, music, fixed layout rules, and a director plan that can be reviewed as a system.

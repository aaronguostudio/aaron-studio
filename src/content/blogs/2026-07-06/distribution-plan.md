# Distribution Plan: Expensive Tokens Won't Save Enterprise AI

## Goal

Find qualified readers who care about AI adoption as an operating problem, not only a model-selection problem. This launch should create thoughtful discussion with builders, product leaders, and operators, then bring the most interested readers to the essay and film on Aaron's site.

## Core Tension

The market keeps celebrating model capability while Anthropic, OpenAI, AWS, and Microsoft are spending heavily on people who can make that capability survive real work. The scarce layer is deployment capacity.

## Launch Hypothesis

A sharp, source-backed FDE claim will earn more qualified engagement than a generic AI commentary post when it names the operational bottleneck and gives practitioners a question they can answer from experience.

## Platform Plan

| Platform | Job | Launch asset and format | CTA | Primary signal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| X | Discovery and conversation | `00-social-cover-v6-square.webp` with the launch post; self-reply article link | Name the deployment bottleneck you see | Qualified replies, profile visits, UTM clicks |
| LinkedIn | Professional credibility | Direct article link with preview, no extra image | Describe where AI deployment stalls in your organization | Target-role comments, out-of-network impressions, UTM clicks |
| Facebook | Relationship-led sharing | Personal copy with direct article-link preview | Tell me where AI has felt useful but has not yet changed the work | Friend comments, shares, UTM clicks |
| Newsletter | Owned-reader follow-up | Short email with article link | Read the full argument and share the deployment failure worth studying | Opens, UTM clicks, replies |

## Experiment Rules

- X starts with the link in a self-reply because it keeps the launch post focused. This is a launch variant, not a claim that direct links are punished.
- LinkedIn uses a direct article link with preview rather than native video so this launch can measure reading intent. A later post can test a native 20-35 second clip.
- Facebook uses a direct link preview and should be intentionally set to `Public` for shareability, unless Aaron prefers a friend-only feedback loop.
- Do not record any social post in `distribution.json` until it is published and has a real canonical URL or post ID.

## Review at 24 Hours and 7 Days

Compare the three UTM paths alongside comment quality. A low-click post that starts strong practitioner conversations is not a failure; it is a relationship asset. A high-click post with no engaged reading or follow-up should not become the default creative.

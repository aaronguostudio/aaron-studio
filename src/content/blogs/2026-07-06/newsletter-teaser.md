Anthropic, OpenAI, AWS, and Microsoft are all moving in the same direction: embedding engineers inside customer organizations to make AI actually work.

That is not a coincidence.

It tells us where the enterprise AI bottleneck moved. The hard part is no longer only model capability. It is deployment: connecting AI to messy workflows, data permissions, governance, user habits, fallback paths, and measurable business outcomes.

I wrote about why forward deployed engineering is becoming the clearest signal of this shift, how it differs from traditional consulting, and what it means for technical people.

The practical lens: ACTOR — Action, Context, Trust, Outcome, Responsibility. The last layer matters because enterprise AI needs a feedback loop, not only a launch.

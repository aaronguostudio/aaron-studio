# Mode Mix

## Set-Level Pacing

The set should balance two loud distribution images with three medium body images and one quieter human-scale bridge image. It should feel more editorial than the previous FDE set: less diagram pressure, fewer glowing paths, and more space.

Allowed visual weights: quiet / medium / loud.

## Image Plan

| Image | Role | Visual mode | Visual weight | Reason | Reuse candidate? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 00-cover | Cover | Editorial Minimal + Real Photo Collage | loud | The cover needs to make the title's critique visible: tokens do not become value without deployment. | yes, if the token/workbench metaphor lands |
| 00-cover-thumbnail | Thumbnail | Editorial Minimal + typography | loud | The thumbnail needs the same world as the cover with readable title text. | no |
| 01-scene-platforms-converge | Body thesis | Editorial Minimal | medium | Show multiple AI platforms converging toward a deployment layer without explaining every vendor. | yes, if clean |
| 02-metaphor-demo-to-operating-loop | Body metaphor | Human-Scale Metaphor | quiet | Give the reader a calmer image for the demo-to-deployment gap. | yes |
| 03-comparison-fde-center-of-gravity | Comparison | Operator Diagram | medium | Compress consulting vs FDE into one handoff line versus one embedded loop. | yes |
| 04-framework-actor-loop | Framework | Operator Diagram + light Glass / Fluent Artifact | medium | Make ACTOR memorable and reusable, with one recursive feedback path. | yes |

## Density Budget

- Narrative/metaphor images: 2 (`00-cover`, `02-metaphor-demo-to-operating-loop`)
- Editorial thesis images: 1 (`01-scene-platforms-converge`)
- Light diagrams: 2 (`03-comparison-fde-center-of-gravity`, `04-framework-actor-loop`)
- Dense mechanism diagrams: 0

## Style Fatigue Guardrail

Use Glass / Fluent Artifact only as a light accent in the ACTOR framework and possibly a subtle layer in the cover. Do not use glowing route lines as the default AI metaphor. No more than two images may contain visible glass or route-line effects.

# FDE Deployment Editorial

## Base Visual Language

Soft Glass Narrative: flat editorial composition first, human-scale metaphor second, restrained liquid-glass material third.

Baseline: Soft Glass Narrative, using the 2026-06-20 AI operating system image set as the reference for density, restraint, and human-scale metaphor.

## Article Thesis

Enterprise AI value is not created by buying more expensive tokens. It is created when models become reliable operating capacity inside real workflows.

## Intended Reader Emotion

Sober, curious, and practically energized. The reader should feel that enterprise AI deployment is not mysterious, but it is much more operational than a demo.

## Real-World Anchor

Workbenches, paper artifacts, runbooks, approval stamps, data permission folders, embedded engineers, quiet operating loops, and clean framework cards.

## Data Layer Meaning

Data and AI should appear as subtle connective material: a cyan thread, a translucent layer, a small green checkpoint, or a quiet loop. The data layer should never dominate the human or operational scene.

## Palette

- Ink graphite for depth.
- Warm white paper and desk surfaces.
- Soft cyan for model/context traces.
- Warm amber for human intent and value.
- Soft green for trust, review, and operating readiness.
- Muted coral only for friction or boundary cues.

Avoid dominant dark-blue AI palettes, purple-blue gradients, beige-only creator styling, and neon glow.

## Recurring Motifs

- Expensive token objects: used only in the cover/thumbnail to carry the title conflict.
- Workbench or paper surface: the deployment layer where value becomes operational.
- Green checkpoint: trust and review.
- Cyan thread: intelligence connected to a workflow.
- Large clean tiles: framework/operator artifacts.

## Image Set Rules

- One visual predicate per image.
- Keep body images calmer than the cover.
- Use diagrams only for the comparison and ACTOR framework.
- Use ACTOR letters as large readable elements, not small decorative labels.
- No logos or vendor marks.
- No fake UI text except approved thumbnail headline and ACTOR letters.

## Anti-Patterns

- Platform convergence shown as roads, landscapes, desk maps, gates, and icons all at once.
- Consulting vs FDE shown as a crowded slide.
- ACTOR tiles filled with icon clusters.
- Every image using the same glowing line or glass ribbon.
- Generic AI brain, robot, cloud, abstract network, or stock office scene.

## Quality Checklist

- The image idea is readable in one sentence.
- The main subject is clear at mobile width.
- There are no tiny labels, fake UI, logos, or clutter.
- The image supports the local paragraph rather than summarizing the whole article.
- The full set has rhythm: loud cover, medium thesis, quiet metaphor, clean comparison, clean framework.

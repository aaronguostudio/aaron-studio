# Prompt: 00-cover-v3-controlled-mix

## Role

Style probe for cover.

## Visual Mode

Editorial Minimal + Real Photo Collage.

## Visual Weight

loud

## Style Family

Controlled Mix: Enterprise Editorial Workbench.

## Positive Prompt

A premium editorial business magazine cover image, 16:9. A dark graphite enterprise workbench with three clear zones: on the left, a small pile of expensive abstract token objects, polished and inert; in the center, a visible gap; on the right, a practical deployment surface made of a blank runbook, one green review stamp, and a simple paper loop. One human hand draws a single amber bridge line across the gap. Use realistic paper and metal texture, warm amber edge light, soft cyan thread only as a subtle background trace. Strong negative space, quiet drama, no explanation overload.

## Negative Prompt

No text, no logos, no company marks, no dashboards, no charts, no robots, no brain icon, no cloud icon, no sci-fi HUD, no city skyline, no dense workflow map, no tiny labels, no fake UI, no clutter, no generic stock meeting.

## Mode Constraints

One visual predicate only: expensive tokens become useful only when bridged into a deployment loop.

## Density Budget

Cover probe. Editorial and atmospheric, but still clean.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Avoid the previous set's over-explanation and avoid making the cover visually identical to the first body image.

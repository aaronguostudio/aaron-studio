# Prompt: 02-metaphor-demo-to-operating-loop-v2

## Role

Body metaphor image after "The Demo-To-Deployment Gap".

## Visual Mode

Human-Scale Metaphor.

## Visual Weight

quiet

## Positive Prompt

A calm human-scale editorial scene, 16:9. A desk in warm low light. On the left sits a small translucent glass cube representing a polished AI demo, simple and attractive but isolated. On the right sits a practical paper operating loop: three blank cards arranged in a circle, a permission folder, a runbook notebook, and one green review stamp. A single subtle cyan thread connects the cube to the loop. Plenty of negative space, soft paper texture, restrained liquid-glass accent only on the demo cube. The mood is quiet and thoughtful, not futuristic.

## Negative Prompt

No dashboard, no UI screen, no fake text, no tiny labels, no glowing route maze, no full system map, no sci-fi HUD, no business people posing, no clutter.

## Mode Constraints

One visual predicate only: a demo becomes useful when connected to an operating loop.

## Density Budget

Narrative/metaphor image, restful body pacing.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Avoid overusing glowing route lines and glass gates; this should be calmer than the first version.

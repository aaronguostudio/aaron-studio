# Prompt: 00-video-cover-v6-fde

## Role

Video / social cover candidate focused on FDE.

## Visual Mode

Editorial Minimal + Operator Diagram

## Visual Weight

loud

## Style Family

Infographic Editorial

## Positive Prompt

Create a bold 16:9 YouTube thumbnail / social cover in premium modern editorial infographic style.

Main headline text must be large and readable:
"WHY FDE"
"IS BACK"

Visual concept:
- Huge "FDE" as the hero visual text, modern sans-serif, black with amber accent.
- Around it, abstract platform modules converge into a deployment loop.
- A small engineer/operator icon stands at the boundary between model and workflow.
- Use warm white paper, crisp black outlines, muted cyan paths, amber action line, green outcome loop.
- Make it feel analytical, sharp, and contemporary.

Text layout:
- "WHY FDE" on top or left.
- "IS BACK" below, large and readable.
- Optional small subtitle: "AI needs deployment".

## Negative Prompt

No real company logos, no fake UI dashboard, no stock-photo people, no robot, no tiny labels, no misspelled text, no ornate serif poster, no watermark.

## Mode Constraints

One hook only: FDE matters because AI needs deployment.

## Density Budget

Thumbnail cover, low element count.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Avoid making it look like a hiring poster or corporate HR graphic.

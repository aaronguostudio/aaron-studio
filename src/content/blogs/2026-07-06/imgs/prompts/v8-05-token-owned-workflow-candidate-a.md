## Positive Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept. Asset type: 16:9 closing blog illustration. One visual predicate: expensive model input becomes value only after passing through a customer-owned workflow that produces a verified action and reusable learning. On a calm warm-white field, a long safety-orange metered paper roll and a few orange token-like discs enter from the left. At center is one substantial graphite mechanical workflow press with a visible brass key inserted by the customer and a compact stack of rule plates inside, communicating ownership without words. From the right side, one verified green operational action moves a real shipment route forward, while one thin cyan-green reusable pattern plate loops back into the press for the next run. Crisp paper-cut geometry, premium editorial print, precise shadows, restrained halftone, generous negative space, visually stronger and more concrete than a checkmark moving a lever.

# Prompt: 00-cover-thumbnail-v2-candidate-b

## Role

YouTube/social thumbnail.

## Visual Mode

Editorial Minimal + typography.

## Visual Weight

loud

## Positive Prompt

YouTube thumbnail, 16:9, bold editorial business style. Dark left third for headline text. Right side shows a premium workbench with expensive abstract tokens, a blank runbook, one green checkpoint stamp, and a clean cyan deployment loop. Warm amber and graphite palette.

LEFT SIDE text, very large and readable:
- Small cyan eyebrow: "AI VALUE"
- Bold white line: "ISN'T"
- Huge amber/gold line: "TOKENS"
- Small grey line: "it's deployment"

Right side must remain simple and cinematic. No other words or labels.

## Negative Prompt

No logos, no extra text, no fake UI, no unreadable words, no dashboards, no robots, no decorative clutter, no overdone glow.

## Mode Constraints

Only the headline text should appear. Keep it readable at mobile size.

## Density Budget

Distribution thumbnail, low detail behind text.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Avoid generated thumbnail text that becomes crowded or misspelled.

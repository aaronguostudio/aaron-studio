# Prompt: 03-handoff-to-loop-v5-infographic-editorial-set

## Role

Body image for the consulting vs forward deployed engineering section.

## Visual Mode

Operator Diagram

## Visual Weight

medium

## Style Family

Infographic Editorial

## Positive Prompt

Create a premium editorial comparison infographic about traditional consulting versus forward deployed engineering.

Core visual predicate: traditional consulting centers on handoff; FDE compresses the chain into an embedded build loop.

Composition:
- 16:9 landscape.
- Warm white paper background, clean magazine layout, strong spacing.
- Two-column comparison with large visual metaphors, not a dense table.
- Left column: HANDOFF, represented by a roadmap document ending before implementation.
- Right column: LOOP, represented by a circular embedded build cycle connecting user, system, review, and outcome.
- A small arrow shows the center of gravity moving from document handoff to operating loop.
- Use black outlines, muted cyan flow, amber action accents, green review/output marks.
- Make it feel like a smart business magazine infographic rather than a consulting slide.

Text guidance:
- Use only big labels: HANDOFF, LOOP, ROADMAP, BUILD, REVIEW, OUTCOME.
- No bullet list, no paragraphs.

## Negative Prompt

No full comparison table, no tiny icons, no many rows, no fake consultancy deck, no corporate silhouettes, no dashboards, no logos, no dense arrows.

## Mode Constraints

One predicate only: handoff becomes loop.

## Density Budget

Two-column light comparison, no more than six major elements.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Do not compress the full consulting comparison table into the image.

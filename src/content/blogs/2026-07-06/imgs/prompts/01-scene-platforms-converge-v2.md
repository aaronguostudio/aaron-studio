# Prompt: 01-scene-platforms-converge-v2

## Role

Body thesis image after "The Pattern Is Deployment, Not Services".

## Visual Mode

Editorial Minimal.

## Visual Weight

medium

## Positive Prompt

A clean high-end editorial illustration, 16:9. Four simple abstract source marks near the top edge, each a different muted shape without logos, send four faint cyan threads downward. The threads converge into one grounded deployment layer: a warm workbench strip with a blank runbook, one soft green checkpoint, and one amber pencil line. Large negative space. Flat editorial composition with subtle paper texture. The image should communicate that different AI platforms are all moving toward deployment capability.

## Negative Prompt

No roads, no landscape, no vendor logos, no platform icons, no city skyline, no desk map, no dashboards, no fake text, no tiny labels, no chart wall, no dense arrows, no sci-fi HUD.

## Mode Constraints

One visual predicate only: separate platform streams converge into one deployment layer.

## Density Budget

Narrative/metaphor image. No more than four source marks and three foreground objects.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

The previous version was too cluttered because it combined roads, landscape, symbols, desk objects, workflow maps, gates, and industry artifacts.

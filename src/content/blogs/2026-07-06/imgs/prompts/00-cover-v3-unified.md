# Prompt: 00-cover-v3-unified

## Role

Style probe for cover.

## Visual Mode

Editorial Minimal + Real Photo Collage.

## Visual Weight

loud

## Style Family

Unified: Enterprise Editorial Workbench.

## Positive Prompt

A premium editorial cover image, 16:9, in one coherent enterprise workbench visual world. Dark graphite table, warm amber light, realistic paper, stone, metal, and glass. Expensive abstract token objects sit on a small tray, separated from a deployment loop made of blank cards, a runbook, and a green stamp. A single pencil bridge line connects the two. The style should feel like a physical artifact photograph from a high-end business magazine, with restrained graphic overlay and no visual elements outside this workbench world.

## Negative Prompt

No text, no logos, no dashboard, no fake UI, no road map, no abstract network background, no sci-fi glow, no office people, no labels, no clutter.

## Mode Constraints

This is the unified style baseline: everything should feel like part of the same physical editorial artifact world.

## Density Budget

Loud cover probe, but low element count.

## Prior Failure To Avoid

Avoid both generic AI art and random mixed styles.

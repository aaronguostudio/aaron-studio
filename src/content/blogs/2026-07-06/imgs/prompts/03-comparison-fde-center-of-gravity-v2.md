# Prompt: 03-comparison-fde-center-of-gravity-v2

## Role

Comparison image after "Consulting Is Not The Villain".

## Visual Mode

Operator Diagram.

## Visual Weight

medium

## Positive Prompt

A clean operator-grade comparison diagram, 16:9, no readable labels. Two horizontal lanes on a warm white background. Top lane: a long segmented handoff chain made of five simple graphite blocks connected by thin grey lines, ending far to the right. Bottom lane: one compact embedded loop wrapping around a single customer workflow block, with a small green checkpoint and one amber build mark inside the loop. Strong spacing, large shapes, very low density, subtle glass accent on the embedded loop only. The visual idea is center of gravity: handoff chain versus embedded build loop.

## Negative Prompt

No text labels, no fake words, no icons inside every node, no detailed artifacts below the comparison, no chart wall, no dashboard, no tiny arrows, no dense flowchart, no logos.

## Mode Constraints

One visual predicate only: FDE compresses handoff into embedded building.

## Density Budget

Light diagram. Maximum two lanes, five top nodes, one bottom loop.

One loud cover, three medium explanatory images, and two quiet narrative/closing images. Shared palette and print texture create cohesion; composition changes by role.

## Density Budget

- Narrative / metaphor: 5 including cover.
- Light diagrams: 1.
- Dense mechanism diagrams: 0.
- Loud images: cover and thumbnail only.

## Style Fatigue Guardrail

- Token stacks appear only in the cover.
- Card rows appear only in ACTOR.
- Strong loop arrows appear only in ACTOR; the FDE and closing images use narrow field-note returns.
- The consulting comparison uses one broad feedback belt, but no versus divider or scorecard.
- Halftone is an accent, never a full dirty texture.
- Glass and glow do not appear in this set.

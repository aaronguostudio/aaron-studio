# Editorial Scorecard

## Editorial Contract

- Reader: a product, technology, or finance leader evaluating an agentic workspace for governed knowledge work.
- Reader's job to be done: decide what to pilot, what to measure, and where human review and workflow ownership must remain.
- One-sentence promise: an evidence-backed comparison and a four-plane test that separates launch breadth from operational readiness.
- Opening scene or bottleneck: two visible deliverables - a sourced 10-slide ChatGPT Work deck and a Cowork expense workbook generated from 100+ receipts with exceptions flagged.
- Original contribution: the comparison is about ownership of the context, execution, artifact, and control planes, then applies those planes to finance.
- Scope boundary: no long-duration personal test of ChatGPT Work, no model benchmark, no audited productivity claim; community anecdotes remain labeled.
- Success hypothesis: timely comparison + proven finance topic + screenshots + portable operator framework should produce qualified reading and discussion.

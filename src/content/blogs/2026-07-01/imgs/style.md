# Visual Style Brief: 一个人的项目

## Base Language

Soft Glass Narrative

## Post-Specific Style Name

Accountable Owner Loop

## Baseline

Soft Glass Narrative, using the 2026-06-20 AI operating system image set as the reference for density, restraint, and human-scale metaphor.

## Article Thesis

AI lowers execution cost, so software collaboration must reorganize around stronger end-to-end ownership, clearer team boundaries, and better evidence systems.

## Reader Should Feel

Clear, grounded, slightly challenged, operationally energized, and confident that this is a practical management shift rather than an abstract AI slogan.

## Human-Scale Anchor

A real project owner reviewing work at a calm desk or team workspace, with handoff stations, review gates, and evidence artifacts shown as concrete objects rather than sci-fi dashboards.

## AI / Glass Layer

AI appears as restrained soft cyan threads, translucent glass work cards, and quiet task loops around the owner. It extends execution but does not dominate the scene.

## Data Layer Meaning

Green gates represent review, verification, and QA evidence. Muted coral boundary lines represent architectural, security, and ownership limits. Warm amber lines represent human judgment and intent.

## Palette

Ink graphite and warm white as the base, soft cyan for AI delegation, warm amber for human intent, soft green for evidence and review, muted coral for risk boundaries, and dusty violet used sparingly for process memory.

## Image Mix

6 total generated assets after candidate selection: clean cover, thumbnail, and 4 body illustrations.

- 3 narrative/metaphor body images
- 1 light framework diagram
- 0 dense mechanism diagrams

## Recurring Motifs

- A single accountable owner as the human anchor
- Soft cyan AI threads moving work cards
- Green review gates
- Muted coral boundary rails
- Warm white evidence pages, test sheets, and logs
- Amber line of human intent

## Image Set Rules

- Keep the cover atmospheric and editorial, with no text.
- Keep the thumbnail visually consistent with the cover but reserve a dark left zone for large readable Chinese title text.
- Body images should feel like pauses inside the essay, not dashboards.
- Use one idea per image; avoid tiny UI labels and fake text.
- Prefer people, desks, project rooms, paper artifacts, gates, loops, and boundary rails over abstract networks.
- The AI layer must feel useful and quiet, not magical.

## Anti-Patterns

- Generic robot, brain, neural network, glowing orb, or sci-fi command center
- Dense chart walls, fake code screens, or tiny unreadable UI
- Purple/blue AI glow dominating the image
- Stock-photo business meetings
- Heroic lone genius imagery
- Any visual that implies QA/team collaboration is obsolete

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] The image can be described in one sentence.
- [ ] It has a human-scale anchor or strong metaphor.
- [ ] The AI layer is secondary unless this is the framework image.
- [ ] It has enough negative space.
- [ ] It avoids fake UI text and chart clutter.
- [ ] It belongs to the same series as the other images.
- [ ] It makes ownership, boundary, review, or evidence easier to understand.

AI coding is not just making developers faster.

It is collapsing the old software delivery loop: requirements, design, implementation, testing, docs, and review evidence.

More of that work now fits inside one owner loop.

That does not mean solo work. It means single ownership inside shared team boundaries.

The new question is not only "which role owns this step?"

It is: who owns this end to end, what boundaries make it safe, and what evidence lets us ship?

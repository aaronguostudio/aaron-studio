Have you heard about this shift in the tech world? They’re calling it the transition from the Attention Economy to the Intention Economy. 

Now, the attention economy — that’s all about social media the way we typically experience it. It’s designed to grab your attention, keep you scrolling, and, in the end, sell that engagement to advertisers. But here’s where it gets interesting: the intention economy flips the script. It’s focused on optimizing for your goals instead of just the platform’s metrics.

Did you know that researchers over at Cambridge are raising some serious red flags? They’re saying that AI is about to start picking up on your intentions as they form, and guess what? It might just sell that info to advertisers... crazy, right? We're heading into an arms race, folks! The big question is, will you create your own system to manage this... or just let someone else do it for you?

# 03-metaphor-work-leaves-desk-v2 Prompt

16:9 body illustration.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.
Density class: narrative/metaphor.

Create a premium flat editorial illustration, warm and modern, with restrained liquid-glass accents.

Section idea: once the pattern worked in coding, it escaped the IDE into writing, video, analytics, admin, and daily life.

Visual metaphor: a small quiet desk room with an open door leading into a bright hallway. Beyond the doorway, four simple symbolic objects float lightly: a notebook page, a film strip, a calendar card, and a small chart seedling. A single translucent glass ribbon connects them, with one soft cyan thread running through it.

Mood: discovery, expansion, and lightness. The work is leaving the desk without becoming chaotic.

Composition rules:
- Doorway/open-space metaphor should dominate.
- Four objects maximum beyond the doorway.
- No hub-and-spoke dashboard.
- No readable labels.
- Large negative space.
- Soft warm light, flat editorial shapes.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No SaaS workflow map.
- No many floating panels.
- No dense nodes.
- No tiny UI.
- No fake text.
- No robot, AI brain, or cloud.
- No neon HUD.
- No photorealistic 3D render.

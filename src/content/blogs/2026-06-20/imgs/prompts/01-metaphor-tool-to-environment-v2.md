# 01-metaphor-tool-to-environment-v2 Prompt

16:9 body illustration.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.
Density class: narrative/metaphor.

Create a premium flat editorial illustration with restrained liquid-glass accents. It should feel like a high-end blog illustration, not an infographic or dashboard.

Section idea: AI stopped feeling like a tool and started feeling like an operating system.

Visual metaphor: a quiet worktable in a warm room. On the left side, one small hand tool rests unused on the table, simple and symbolic. Around the room, the environment itself has become softly intelligent: a few papers gently align themselves, a small lamp glows, and an open window reveals a warm amber road outside. A single broad translucent glass ribbon curves through the room like invisible infrastructure embedded in the environment.

AI / glass layer: one soft cyan thread runs through the glass ribbon, and one small green checkpoint appears near the window. Keep the AI layer quiet and secondary.

Composition rules:
- One clear metaphor, not a diagram.
- Large calm areas and negative space.
- Soft flat shapes, gentle shadows, warm editorial palette.
- No readable text on papers.
- No more than three AI/glass accents.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No split-screen comparison diagram.
- No dashboards.
- No chart panels.
- No tiny UI.
- No labels.
- No robots, brains, cloud icons, or neural network imagery.
- No sci-fi HUD.
- No neon glow overload.
- No dense nodes or route maps.

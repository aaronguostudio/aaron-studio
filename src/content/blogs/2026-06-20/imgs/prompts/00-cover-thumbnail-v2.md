# 00-cover-thumbnail-v2 Prompt

16:9 YouTube / social thumbnail.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.

Create a premium 2D flat editorial thumbnail with restrained liquid-glass accents. It should look like the same visual world as `00-cover-v2`: a calm road-trip scene inside a modern generic electric car, warm California sunset, simplified road, phone, and one soft cyan AI thread.

LEFT SIDE: reserve a clean dark ink-graphite area for bold readable title text, left-aligned:
- Small cyan line: "I Gave Codex"
- Large bold white line: "a Task From"
- Largest bold amber line: "a Moving Tesla"
- Small muted warm-white subtitle: "AI became my operating system"

RIGHT SIDE: simplified car window and highway at sunset. A hand holding a phone with only abstract glow. One broad translucent glass ribbon and one thin cyan thread. Keep the right side calm, not busy.

Text rules:
- The left-side headline/subtitle are the only text in the image.
- Text must be correctly spelled and readable at mobile thumbnail size.
- No text on the phone.
- No labels in the glass layer.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No car logo or Tesla emblem.
- No extra text.
- No fake UI.
- No chart panels.
- No dense dashboard.
- No sci-fi HUD.
- No photorealistic car ad.
- No 3D render.
- No cyberpunk neon.

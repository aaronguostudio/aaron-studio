# 00-cover-v2 Prompt

16:9 blog cover illustration.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.

Create a premium flat editorial illustration with soft dimensional depth, not a photorealistic 3D render. The composition should feel modern, calm, human, and easy to understand.

Scene: inside a modern generic electric car at dusk, traveling on a quiet highway toward California. The view is from the passenger side or back seat. A hand holds a phone near the lower center, but the phone screen shows only abstract soft light and one simple cyan thread, no readable UI. Outside the windshield, the road curves into warm amber sunset light.

AI / glass layer: show the AI operating system as a quiet second layer, not as a dashboard. Add one or two broad translucent liquid-glass ribbons reflected in the windshield and side window. A single soft cyan thread and one small green checkpoint can suggest delegated work continuing in the background. Keep these accents sparse and elegant.

Mood: work continues quietly while life remains foregrounded. The reader should feel calm, curious, modern, and slightly amazed.

Composition rules:
- 1 primary subject: the road and hand with phone.
- 1 supporting layer: subtle glass ribbon / cyan thread.
- Large negative space.
- Flat editorial shapes with soft lighting and gentle depth.
- Warm ink graphite, warm white, soft cyan, warm amber, soft green.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No text.
- No logos or visible car brand marks.
- No Tesla emblem.
- No fake UI labels.
- No dense dashboard.
- No chart walls.
- No sci-fi HUD.
- No glowing AI brain, robot, cloud, or neural network.
- No cyberpunk neon.
- No full 3D command center.
- No cluttered floating panels.

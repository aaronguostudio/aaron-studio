# 08-closing-life-foreground-v2 Prompt

16:9 closing body illustration.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.
Density class: narrative/metaphor.

Create a warm flat editorial illustration with very restrained liquid-glass accents.

Section idea: the point of leverage is not to become a machine; it is to make life bigger.

Visual content: a family road-trip scene near golden hour, seen from behind or from a distance. The family has exactly three people total: one adult man, one adult woman, and one young daughter. No son, no second child, no fourth person, no extra silhouettes. The three-person family is near or inside a car, looking toward California hills and an open road. The landscape and human moment dominate. A faint soft cyan thread continues quietly along the horizon, almost invisible, suggesting background AI work. A warm amber road line leads forward.

Mood: calm, human, spacious, optimistic.

Composition rules:
- Life and landscape foregrounded.
- Exactly three visible family members: father, mother, daughter.
- No fourth person and no second child.
- AI layer very quiet.
- No screens as the main subject.
- Large negative space.
- Warm amber and soft green.
- Gentle flat editorial shapes.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No dashboard.
- No phone UI.
- No charts.
- No robot or AI brain.
- No glowing network.
- No sci-fi HUD.
- No clutter.
- No photorealistic 3D render.

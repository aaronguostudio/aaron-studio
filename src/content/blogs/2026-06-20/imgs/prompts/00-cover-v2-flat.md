# 00-cover-v2-flat Prompt

16:9 blog cover illustration.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.

Create a 2D flat editorial illustration with soft liquid-glass accents. It should look like a premium modern magazine illustration, not photography, not a 3D render, not a realistic car ad.

Visual language:
- simplified but elegant shapes;
- clean silhouettes;
- soft gradients used sparingly;
- gentle paper-like and glass-like texture;
- slightly rounded forms;
- calm negative space;
- no photorealistic lighting;
- no cinematic 3D realism.

Scene: inside a modern generic electric car at dusk, traveling on a quiet highway toward California. The view is from passenger side or back seat. A hand holds a phone near the lower center. The phone screen shows only a soft abstract glow and one simple cyan curve, no UI. Outside the windshield, a simplified amber road curves into a warm coastal sunset.

AI / glass layer: one broad translucent glass ribbon floats across the windshield, with one thin cyan thread and one tiny soft green checkpoint. The layer is quiet and symbolic, not a dashboard.

Mood: calm, human, modern, lightly magical. Work continues in the background while life stays foregrounded.

Composition:
- simple large shapes;
- low information density;
- warm amber road and sunset;
- ink graphite car interior;
- soft cyan AI trace;
- warm white glass reflection;
- no more than three glass/system accents.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No photorealism.
- No 3D render.
- No car ad realism.
- No text.
- No logos or visible car brand marks.
- No Tesla emblem.
- No fake UI labels.
- No dense dashboard.
- No chart walls.
- No sci-fi HUD.
- No glowing AI brain, robot, cloud, or neural network.
- No cyberpunk neon.
- No cluttered floating panels.

# 06-metaphor-feedback-garden-v2 Prompt

16:9 body illustration.

Style system: Soft Glass Narrative, post style Roadside Soft Glass OS.
Density class: narrative/metaphor.

Create a premium flat editorial illustration with warm light and restrained liquid-glass accents.

Section idea: self-enhancement means learning from outcomes and feeding lessons into the next run.

Visual metaphor: a small garden on a desk. Published article pages become paper seeds in the soil. Soft metric particles fall like gentle rain, not like a dashboard. A new outline page grows from the garden as a fresh sprout or paper leaf. One subtle glass pane reflects the next content plan as abstract marks, not readable text.

Mood: learning, growth, compounding, calm.

Composition rules:
- Garden metaphor should be primary.
- No analytics dashboard.
- No metric labels.
- One glass pane only.
- Soft cyan and warm amber accents.
- Keep it simple and readable.

Strict anti-patterns:
- No charts.
- No pageview labels.
- No dashboard cards.
- No dense data visualization.
- No fake text.
- No glowing network.
- No 3D render.
- No sci-fi HUD.

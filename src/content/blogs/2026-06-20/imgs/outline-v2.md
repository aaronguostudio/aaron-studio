# Illustration Plan v2: Roadside Soft Glass OS

## Visual System

This v2 outline follows `imgs/style-v2.md`. It intentionally reduces information density from the v1 image set.

The article is a blog essay, not a research deck. Most images should give the reader emotional rhythm, metaphor, and clarity. Only two body images are allowed to behave like diagrams.

**Density rule:** 70 / 20 / 10.

- Narrative/metaphor: cover, body 1, body 2, body 3, body 6, body 7, body 8.
- Light architecture diagram: body 4.
- Clean framework diagram: body 5.
- Dense dashboard/system map: none.

## Cover Image

**Position:** Top of article
**Type:** Soft Glass Editorial scene
**Purpose:** Establish the lived story: work continues quietly while Aaron is on the road with family.
**Visual Content:** A calm, modern electric-car interior at dusk on a highway toward California. A hand holds a phone, but the phone screen is mostly abstract light and a single soft glass thread, not a UI dashboard. Outside the windshield, the road curves into warm amber light. The AI operating system appears as one or two subtle glass ribbons reflected in the window, suggesting work moving in the background. Human life and the road are the primary subject.
**Density:** narrative/metaphor
**Style note:** More editorial illustration than cinematic 3D. Flat-soft shapes, gentle depth, restrained liquid glass. No text, no logo, no dense overlays.
**Filename:** 00-cover-v2.png

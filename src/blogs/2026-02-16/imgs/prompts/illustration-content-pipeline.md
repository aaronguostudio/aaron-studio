The AI Content Pipeline - 1 Input to 8 Outputs

Minimalist hand-drawn line art infographic in Notion style. Clean white background (#FFFFFF).

Layout: Left-to-right flow, starting narrow and fanning out wide.

LEFT (Input):
- A single messy note icon labeled "plan.md" with scribbled lines inside. Slightly tilted. Soft Blue (#A8D4F0) accent.

CENTER (Pipeline - 7 numbered steps as small circles connected by arrows):
- Step nodes in a gentle downward-then-upward curve, each as a small numbered circle (1-7)
- Simple icons next to each: 1-pen, 2-document, 3-picture, 4-globe, 5-chat, 6-film, 7-rocket
- Thin hand-drawn arrows connecting each step
- Steps colored alternating Soft Blue (#A8D4F0) and Sage Green (#B2DFDB)

RIGHT (Outputs - fanning out):
- 8 small output icons arranged in a fan/tree pattern:
  • Blog post (document icon)
  • Chinese translation (globe + document)
  • Cover image (picture frame)
  • 5 illustrations (small grid of pictures)
  • X thread (speech bubble)
  • LinkedIn post (briefcase icon)
  • YouTube script (film icon)
  • Video (play button)
- Each with a tiny label below

PROMINENT LABEL: "1 input → 8 outputs" in bold hand-drawn text, centered below the diagram

COLORS: Black outlines (#1A1A1A) on white. Soft Blue (#A8D4F0), Slate Gray (#B0BEC5), Sage Green (#B2DFDB). No warm colors.

STYLE: Hand-drawn wobble. Doodle icons. Single-weight lines. Clean lettering. Generous whitespace.

Clean composition with generous white space. Simple or no background. Main elements centered.

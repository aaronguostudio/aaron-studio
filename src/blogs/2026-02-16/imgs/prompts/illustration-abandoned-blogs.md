The Quit Cycle - Abandoned Blog Drafts

Minimalist hand-drawn line art infographic in Notion style. Clean white background (#FFFFFF).

Layout: Circular flow diagram showing a repeating cycle, centered on canvas.

CYCLE ELEMENTS (clockwise):
1. A lightbulb icon with label "Get inspired" — in Soft Blue (#A8D4F0)
2. A document icon with "Draft 1, Draft 2, Draft 3" stacked, showing writing progress
3. A calendar icon with months ticking by, label "Life gets busy" — in Slate Gray (#B0BEC5)
4. A wilted/faded document icon with an X mark, label "Abandon" — in Slate Gray
5. A dotted curved arrow looping back to step 1, label "Repeat"

CENTER: Text "The Quit Cycle" in hand-drawn lettering. A small counter showing "× Years" to emphasize duration.

COLORS: Black outlines (#1A1A1A) on white. Soft Blue (#A8D4F0) for the hopeful start. Slate Gray (#B0BEC5) for the fading/quitting stages. Sage Green (#B2DFDB) sparingly. No warm colors.

STYLE: Hand-drawn wobble effect. Simple doodle icons. Single-weight lines. Maximum whitespace. Conceptual, not literal.

Clean composition with generous white space. Simple or no background. Main elements centered.

ASPECT: 16:9

The Hidden 6-Hour Cost of a Blog Post

Minimalist hand-drawn line art infographic in Notion style. Clean white background (#FFFFFF).

Layout: Horizontal stacked bar chart, centered on canvas.

BAR SEGMENTS (left to right, proportional to time):
1. "Writing" — 1.5h — Soft Blue (#A8D4F0) fill — pen icon
2. "Formatting" — 0.5h — Slate Gray (#B0BEC5) fill — document icon
3. "Images" — 1h — Sage Green (#B2DFDB) fill — picture icon
4. "Translation" — 1.5h — Soft Blue (#A8D4F0) fill — globe icon
5. "Publishing" — 0.5h — Slate Gray (#B0BEC5) fill — rocket icon
6. "Social" — 0.5h — Sage Green (#B2DFDB) fill — chat bubble icon

Each segment has a simple hand-drawn icon above it and the time label below. Black outlines on all segments.

TOTAL LABEL: "~6 hours per post" in bold hand-drawn text at the right end, with a hand-drawn exclamation mark.

BOTTOM NOTE: Small text "For someone with a full-time job... these hours don't exist consistently"

COLORS: Black outlines (#1A1A1A) on white. Only Soft Blue (#A8D4F0), Slate Gray (#B0BEC5), and Sage Green (#B2DFDB). No warm colors.

STYLE: Hand-drawn wobble. Clean lettering. Simple icons. Maximum whitespace around the bar.

Clean composition with generous white space. Simple or no background. Main elements centered.

Have you noticed how every few decades, a game-changing tech pops up? Think about it: the camera, the synthesizer, desktop publishing, Auto-Tune... Each time, there’s this big hype about democratizing creativity. They all promise, "Now anyone can create professional-quality stuff!" 

But guess what? The outcome is almost always the same. Sure, these tools make production a breeze, but creating something truly great? Yeah, that’s still tricky. The real challenge was never just the production; it was about having taste, vision, and something meaningful to express. 

Now, we’re seeing the spotlight on AI. And, wow, the buzz is louder than ever!

The Anti-Goal Operating System — Two Systems Compared

STRUCTURE: Two-column comparison framework with connecting principle at bottom

LEFT COLUMN — "Goal OS" (muted, rigid):
- Header: "Goal Operating System"
- Items stacked vertically with rigid rectangular boxes:
  - "5-Year Plan" with timeline icon
  - "Revenue Targets" with dollar icon
  - "Follower Goals" with chart icon
  - "Career Destination" with pin/flag icon
- Each box feels constraining, rigid borders
- Subtle stress marks / tension lines around boxes
- Overall feel: structured but anxious

RIGHT COLUMN — "Process OS" (vibrant, flowing):
- Header: "Process Operating System"
- Items stacked vertically with rounded flowing shapes:
  - "Daily Learning Rituals" with sunrise/book icon

- Subtle growth lines / sprouting elements
- Overall feel: natural and compounding

DIVIDER: Elegant thin vertical line between columns

- Quote: "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."

- Gold (#C9A962) accent line above the quote

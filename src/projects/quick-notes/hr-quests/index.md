HR People Management Tool — Step 1 (Select Process Type)

Build a multi-step HR process wizard web application called "Mawer People Management Tool".

Overall Layout:

Full-width page with a light gray background (#F5F6FA)
Two-column layout: a fixed left sidebar (~240px wide) and a main content area
Left Sidebar:

Light blue-gray background (#EEF1F8)
Centered logo at the top: a circular icon with the letter "M" and decorative arc lines in teal and orange
Label below logo: "People Management Tool" in small gray text
Vertical step indicator with 4 steps:
Select Process Type — "Choose the type of HR process" (active: dark navy filled circle with white number)
Employee Details — "Provide employee information" (inactive: outlined circle)
Create Request — "Review and submit your request" (inactive)
Summary — "Overview of completed actions" (inactive)
Each step has a bold title and a subtitle; inactive steps are grayed out
Steps are connected by a vertical line
Top Header (main area):

Bold title: "Mawer People Management Tool"
Subtitle: "A centralized tool to manage employee onboarding, offboarding, and changes to roles, titles, and departments."
Top-right: user avatar (circular photo) + name "Aaron Guo" + dropdown chevron
A horizontal divider below the header
Main Content Area:

Step progress label: "Step 1/4" in small gray text
Section heading: "Select Process Type" (bold, large)
Section subtext: "Choose the type of HR process you want to initiate"
A horizontal divider
Three equal-width selectable cards in a row, each with:
Light gray background with rounded corners and a subtle border
Centered circular icon (gray background) with a relevant arrow/direction icon
Bold card title below the icon
Short description text in gray
Cards: Onboarding ("Set up a new employee with all necessary accounts and equipment"), Offboarding ("Process employee departure and revoke access to systems"), Role Change ("Update employee role, title, or department information")
On hover/select: card gets a highlighted border (blue) and slight background tint
Bottom-right: a blue "Next" button (#3B6FE8, rounded, disabled until a card is selected)
State: No card selected by default. Selecting a card highlights it and enables the Next button.

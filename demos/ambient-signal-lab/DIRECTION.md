# Ambient Signal Engine — selected direction

## Reference deconstruction

The Endel web player's free Focus, Sleep, and Relax modes establish a useful
contract: black field, sparse line work, one clear visual family per mental
state, and short muted video loops separated from the sound engine. The loops
observed in the browser were approximately 18 seconds for Focus, 10 seconds for
Sleep, and 20 seconds for Relax.

Borrow:

- low visual urgency;
- short loop assets that can support long sessions;
- distinct geometry for different cognitive jobs;
- sound and visual systems that can evolve independently.

Do not copy:

- its exact grid, moon/constellation, waveform compositions, icons, assets, or
  timing;
- the grayscale-only limit;
- pre-rendered MP4 as the editable source of truth.

## Selected treatment

Build one deterministic Canvas engine with three authored scene families:

1. **Signal Loom / Focus** — perspective scaffold, modular panes, routed paths,
   and one slow attention sweep.
2. **Moon Tides / Sleep** — orbital parallax, crescent light, seeded stars, and
   a gently rotating constellation.
3. **Breath Field / Relax** — phase-coupled waves, travelling particles, and a
   central breathing envelope.

The quality advantage is systemic: mathematically seamless loops, responsive
web preview, deterministic frame rendering, reusable seeds and palettes, and a
single implementation shared with Remotion.

## Explicit exclusions

- no copied media or source code;
- no beat-to-position jitter, shake, or random geometry changes;
- no CSS animation in Remotion;
- no hover or pointer dependency in the rendered composition;
- no claim that the prototype is production-ready before entry/peak/exit and
  motion QA pass.
